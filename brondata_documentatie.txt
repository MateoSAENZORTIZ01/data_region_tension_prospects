The .csv files contain the source data from the grid operators regarding the capacity map.
Here we briefly explain what you can find in them and what the provided information stands for.

The files are semicolon-separated and use a comma as a decimal separator.

congestie_pc6 (congestion_pc6)
	postcode (postcode / zip code)
		6-character long postcode that the provided information relates to
	afname (kleurcode) (consumption / demand - color code)
		-1	No information available
		0	Transparent
		1	Yellow
		2	Orange
		3	Red
	opwek (kleurcode) (generation / supply - color code)
		-1	No information available
		0	Transparent
		1	Yellow
		2	Orange
		3	Red
	voedingsgebied_id (supply_area_id)
		Unique identifier of the regional grid operator's area to which the postcode area can be linked
	voedingsgebied_naam (supply_area_name)
		Name of the supply area as shown on the map
	tennet_id (tennet_id)
		Unique identifier of the TenneT substation to which the postcode area can be linked 
	RNB_postcode (RGO_postcode)
		The Regional Grid Operator (RNB) managing (the largest part of) the postcode area
		
voedingsgebieden (supply_areas)
	voedingsgebied_id (supply_area_id)
		Unique identifier of the regional grid operator's supply area that the provided information relates to
	aanwezige_transportcapaciteit_invoeding (available_transmission_capacity_feed-in)
		Maximum feed-in capacity the grid can handle in this area, in MW
	aanwezige_transportcapaciteit_afname (available_transmission_capacity_consumption)
		Maximum consumption capacity the grid can handle in this area, in MW
	benodigde_transportcapaciteit_invoeding (required_transmission_capacity_feed-in)
		Transmission capacity required for feed-in in this area during peak times to execute transmission agreements, in MW
	benodigde_transportcapaciteit_afname (required_transmission_capacity_consumption)
		Transmission capacity required for consumption in this area during peak times to execute transmission agreements, in MW
	unieke_verzoeken_invoeding (unique_requests_feed-in)
		Number of unique requests (feed-in)
	unieke_verzoeken_afname (unique_requests_consumption)
		Number of unique requests (consumption)
	wachtrij_invoeding (queue_feed-in)
		Queue in capacity, in MW (feed-in)
	wachtrij_afname (queue_consumption)
		Queue in capacity, in MW (consumption)
	RNB (RGO)
		The Regional Grid Operator managing the supply area
	info (info)
		Additional information that the regional grid operator wants to provide for this supply area

tennetgebieden (tennet_areas)
	aanwezige_transportcapaciteit_invoeding (available_transmission_capacity_feed-in)
		Maximum feed-in capacity the grid can handle in this area, in MW
	aanwezige_transportcapaciteit_afname (available_transmission_capacity_consumption)
		Maximum consumption capacity the grid can handle in this area, in MW
	benodigde_transportcapaciteit_invoeding (required_transmission_capacity_feed-in)
		Transmission capacity required for feed-in in this area during peak times to execute transmission agreements, in MW
	benodigde_transportcapaciteit_afname (required_transmission_capacity_consumption)
		Transmission capacity required for consumption in this area during peak times to execute transmission agreements, in MW
	unieke_verzoeken_invoeding (unique_requests_feed-in)
		Number of unique requests (feed-in)
	unieke_verzoeken_afname (unique_requests_consumption)
		Number of unique requests (consumption)
	wachtrij_invoeding (queue_feed-in)
		Queue in capacity, in MW (feed-in)
	wachtrij_afname (queue_consumption)
		Queue in capacity, in MW (consumption)
	congestiegebied (congestion_area)
		The relevant TenneT congestion area that the information relates to
	info (info)
		Additional information that TenneT wants to provide for this area
		
tennetcongestie (tennet_congestion)
	tennet_id (tennet_id)
		Unique identifier of the TenneT substation
	congestiegebied_afname (congestion_area_consumption)
		The TenneT congestion area to which this substation can be linked for consumption
	congestiegebied_opwek (congestion_area_generation)
		The TenneT congestion area to which this substation can be linked for feed-in
	afname (kleurcode) (consumption / demand - color code)
		-1	No information available
		0	Transparent
		1	Yellow
		2	Orange
		3	Red
	opwek (kleurcode) (generation / supply - color code)
		-1	No information available
		0	Transparent
		1	Yellow
		2	Orange
		3	Red

projecten (projects)
	projectnaam (project_name)
		Name and/or brief description of the grid expansion
	gebied_id (area_id)
		The supply or congestion area where the project is being executed
	kwartaal (quarter)
		Quarter of delivery/completion
	jaar (year)
		Year of delivery/completion
	beschrijving (description)
		Source of the project
	einddatum (end_date)
		Expected date of delivery/completion
	netbeheerder (grid_operator)
		The (regional) grid operator overseeing this project
